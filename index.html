Weihan Xu

I am a recent graduate from Duke University. I am interested in multimodal learning in creative applications.

I have been working with Dr. Julian McAuley, Dr. Taylor Berg-Kirkpatrick, Dr. Hao-Wen Dong, Dr. Paul Liang, and Dr. Shlomo Dubnov on multimodal pretraining and creative content generation.

I was fortunate to work with Dr. Cynthia Rudin on interpretable music analysis, and Dr. Pardis Emami-Naeini on human-AI interaction.

Previously, I did my undergraduate at University of Michigan with a double major in computer science (honors) and data science, where I worked with Dr. Sardar Ansari and Dr. Kayvan Najarian on time series data analysis in clinical settings. I also worked with Dr. Gongjun Xu on statistical analysis of education assessment data.

I began studying music at six and have training in piano, violin, and the French horn.
